Sunway Group Sustainable Development Goals Initiatives

Initiative 1 - Food CPR: Compost. Plant. Reduce

The #ZeroFoodWastage initiative is part of Sunway's commitment to sustainability. Sunway is a leading Malaysian conglomerate with a strong focus on environmental and social responsibility. The company has a number of other sustainability initiatives, including a renewable energy program, a water conservation program, and a waste management program.

The #ZeroFoodWastage initiative is a valuable contribution to the fight against food waste. It helps to ensure that food is not wasted, which in turn helps to feed the hungry, promote sustainable agriculture, and mitigate climate change.

Here are some additional details about the #ZeroFoodWastage initiative

  • It was launched in 2017 by Sunway Hotels & Resorts.
  • It has since expanded to include other Sunway businesses, such as Sunway Resort City and Sunway University.
  • In 2022, Sunway Hotels & Resorts donated over 100,000 meals to Kechara Soup Kitchen Society.
  • The initiative is estimated to have prevented over 1,000 tonnes of food waste from being sent to landfills.

Through the Food CPR initiative, Sunway Pyramid envisions a future where sustainable practices become ingrained in our daily lives, creating a positive impact on the community and the planet.

How to Participate in the Food CPR Initiative

  • 1. When: Join workshops and events held on weekends. Check the schedule for upcoming sessions.
  • 2. Where: Look for the dedicated composting bins located near food courts and dining areas.
  • 3. How: Follow the instructions provided on the bins for proper food waste segregation.
