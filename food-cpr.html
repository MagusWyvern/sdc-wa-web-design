Sunway Pyramid Sustainable Development Goals Initiatives

Initiative 1 - Food CPR: Compost. Plant. Reduce

Sunway Pyramid's Food CPR initiative aims to reduce food waste, promote sustainable farming practices, and raise awareness about the importance of composting. Through this initiative, Sunway Pyramid collaborates with local communities, restaurants, and food vendors to manage food waste efficiently and turn it into compost.

Compost produced from food waste is used to enrich soil in community gardens and green spaces. This supports urban farming and contributes to a greener environment while reducing the carbon footprint of the mall and its partners.

Visitors to Sunway Pyramid are encouraged to participate by segregating food waste at dedicated composting bins and learning about sustainable gardening practices at workshops and events organized as part of the Food CPR campaign.

Through the Food CPR initiative, Sunway Pyramid envisions a future where sustainable practices become ingrained in our daily lives, creating a positive impact on the community and the planet.

How to Participate in the Food CPR Initiative

  • 1. When: Join workshops and events held on weekends. Check the schedule for upcoming sessions.
  • 2. Where: Look for the dedicated composting bins located near food courts and dining areas.
  • 3. How: Follow the instructions provided on the bins for proper food waste segregation.
