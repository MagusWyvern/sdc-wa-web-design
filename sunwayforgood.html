Sunway Group Sustainable Development Goals Initiatives

Initiative 4 - Sunway for Good

Sunway for Good is a corporate social responsibility (CSR) programme by the Sunway Group. The programme was launched in 2017 with the aim of "creating a better tomorrow for all".

Sunway for Good focuses on four key areas:

  • Education: The programme provides scholarships, educational resources, and training to underprivileged children and youth.
  • Healthcare: The programme supports cancer awareness and research, and provides access to healthcare for the underprivileged.
  • Sustainability: The programme promotes sustainable living practices and environmental protection.
  • Community development: The programme supports community development initiatives that promote social inclusion and economic empowerment.

Sunway for Good has a number of impact measurement tools in place to track its progress. These tools include surveys, focus groups, and impact evaluations. The programme has also been recognised by a number of international organisations, including the United Nations Global Compact and the World Economic Forum

Sunway for Good is an example of how a large corporation can use its resources to make a positive impact on society. The programme has helped to improve the lives of thousands of people, and it is a testament to the Sunway Group's commitment to social responsibility.

Aligned with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

Goal 1: No Poverty

This initiative has raised over RM1 million for charity. The collected money will be used to do all sorts of charity programmes planned by Sunway.

Goal 3: Good Health And Well Being

Programmes such as Sunway Child and Adolescent Behavioural Health talk raises awareness of child and adolescent mental health issues.

Goal 4: Quality Education

Sunway contributes to the needy by providing students with necessities during the Back To School period.

Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

The Sunway CPR and AED workshops teaches basic life-saving skills to the public to provide them with knowledge on how to deal with emergency situations.
